Individual

The Feedback Agreement is designed for the Decentralized Identity Foundation to invite individual contributors to DIF Working Groups, free of charge. The contributions are provided on an individual basis, not as a representative of an organization or company (No full time employment). Regardless of freelance status, check with legal department of your clients to make sure this agreement would be actionable.
