Join

the Decentralized Identity Foundation

Collaborate with a global community to build open standards and open source technical components for decentralized identity. Companies, research clusters, most non-profits, and government agencies can sign up directly as described below. Please note that all variants of the DIF agreement grants Intellectual Property Rights over all DIF activity to DIF. If you sign our membership agreement, you attest that you control and can waive all intellectual property rights over anything you share in DIF meetings or on DIF repositories.

Open-Source foundations and other member organizations, please get in touch via membership@identity.foundation prior to filling out the application.

Choose the Membership that's right for you

Contributor

For startups, small organizations, academics, or non-profit organizations. (Free membership.)

If there are any concerns about your ability to sign an intellectual property rights agreement on behalf of your organization, please contact membership@identity.foundation.

Associate

For organizations and government agencies that wish to support DIF's mission and participate as organizational members. We encourage funded and revenue-generating organizations of any size to join as Associate members.

If there are any concerns about your ability to sign an intellectual property rights agreement on behalf of your organization, please contact membership@identity.foundation.

Feedback Only

Personal capacity only. Feedback and review comments.

✅ The only rights this grants: submitting feedback / review comments on published specifications.

⚠️Employed or otherwise IP-bound to an org? You are ineligible to give unencumbered Feedback, and should apply as a Contributor or Associate membership instead.

⚠️ If you are a freelancer or sole founder, please use “Contributor” with your LLC/org email instead.

DIF is entirely supported by member contributions.

If you are able to support DIF with an Associate Membership, we encourage you to do so.
